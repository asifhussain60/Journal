### Sunday, April 26 — Delaware Day Trip 🏛️
- **8:30am** — Light breakfast at hotel, hit the road. Head south-west toward Pennsylvania's Brandywine Valley (~1 hr 45 min via NJ Turnpike & I-95 South).
- **10:30am** — **Longwood Gardens** (1001 Longwood Rd, Kennett Square, PA). One of the world's great botanical gardens — 1,077 acres of spring blooms, fountains, conservatories, and scenic walking paths. Allow ~2 hours.
  - *Tip: Book timed-entry tickets at longwoodgardens.org. Opens 10 AM Sundays. Wear comfortable walking shoes.*
- **12:45pm** — **Scenic Brandywine Valley Drive** — wind through Route 52 & Creek Road. Rolling hills, historic estates, stone bridges, and creek-side views. One of the prettiest drives in the Mid-Atlantic (~30 min to Christiana).
- **1:15pm** — **Christiana Mall** (132 Christiana Mall, Newark, DE). Delaware's premier mall — tax-free shopping! Nordstrom, Apple, Coach, Michael Kors, H&M, and 130+ stores. Grab lunch at the food court or sit-down restaurants inside.
  - *Tip: Tax-free shopping saves 6–7% on everything. Plan ~2.5 hours. Mall closes at 6 PM on Sundays.*
- **4:00pm** — **Brandywine Creek State Park** (41 Adams Dam Rd, Wilmington, DE, ~15 min north of mall). Quick scenic walk along Brandywine Creek — gentle trails, creek views, and a beautiful stone-arch bridge. Indian Springs Trail (1.2 mi loop) is flat and ideal for a 30–40 min stroll.
- **4:45pm** — Drive to Red Bank, NJ via I-95 & NJ Turnpike (~1 hr 30 min). Arrive with time to stroll charming downtown Red Bank before dinner.
- **7:00pm** — **Dinner at The Melting Pot** — 2 Bridge Ave, Red Bank. Fondue date night — cheese, entrées, and chocolate fondue. The perfect end to a big day out. Book ahead.
