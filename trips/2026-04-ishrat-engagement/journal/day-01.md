# Day 1 — Monday, April 20
## She Lands

**Planned:** Ishrat arrives on UA 147, landing at EWR around 2:55 PM. Drive to Home2 Suites in New Brunswick. Welcome dinner at Mithaas.

**What Actually Happened:**

I have been counting days like a man who forgot what patience feels like. Seven hours in the air, customs, baggage, and then she walks out through those sliding doors at Newark and the whole week becomes real.

The drive to New Brunswick was quiet. Not awkward quiet. Tired quiet. She leaned the seat back and closed her eyes somewhere around the Turnpike, and I let her. London is five hours ahead. By the time we sat down at Mithaas, it was midnight in her body.

She ordered light. I ordered too much, the way I always do when I am trying to make something feel like a celebration. We were back at the hotel by eight. She was asleep before I finished unpacking.

Nine days. That is what we have. I intend to use every one of them.
